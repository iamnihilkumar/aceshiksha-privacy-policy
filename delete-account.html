Delete Your AceShiksha Account

AceShiksha — Educational App for Indian Schools (Classes 6–11)

You can request deletion of your AceShiksha account and all associated data at any time. Please follow the steps below.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Step 1Open the AceShiksha app on your Android device.
  2. Step 2Log in with your registered email and password.
  3. Step 3Tap the Settings icon in the app.
  4. Step 4Scroll down and tap "Delete Account".
  5. Step 5Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Alternatively, you can request account deletion by emailing us directly:

Email: nikhilsharma75612@gmail.com
Subject: Account Deletion Request — AceShiksha
Please include your registered email address in the message.
We will process your request within 30 days.

What Data Gets Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your profile (name, email, role, class level)
  • All quiz attempts and scores
  • All game scores (Maze, Car Race, History Timeline)
  • Leaderboard entries
  • Lesson progress and AI feedback history
  • Your Firebase Authentication account

⚠️ This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

Data Retention After Deletion

All personal data is deleted within 30 days of your request. No personal data is retained after this period.

Anonymized, non-identifiable statistical data (such as aggregate quiz completion counts) may be retained for app improvement purposes.
