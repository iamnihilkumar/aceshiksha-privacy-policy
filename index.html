Privacy Policy — AceShiksha

Last Updated: April 13, 2026  |  Effective Date: April 13, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to AceShiksha ("the App"), an educational application designed for students in Classes 6–11 and their teachers in Indian schools. AceShiksha is developed and operated by an individual developer ("we", "us", or "our").

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, with whom we share it, and what rights you have. By using AceShiksha, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

If you are a parent or guardian of a child using this App, please read this policy carefully. If you are a teacher or school administrator using this App on behalf of students, you are responsible for obtaining appropriate consents as required by your institution.

2. Who This App Is For

AceShiksha is designed for:

  • Students in Classes 6–11 (approximately ages 11–17)
  • Teachers and educators in Indian schools

The App may be used by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 without verifiable parental or guardian consent, or without authorization through their school or teacher. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal data without proper consent, please contact us immediately at nikhilsharma75612@gmail.com and we will delete that data promptly.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you register an account, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Password (stored in encrypted form via Firebase Authentication — we never see your raw password)
  • Role: Student or Teacher
  • Class level (for students, e.g., Class 6, 7, 8, 9, 10, or 11)

3.2 Information Generated Through App Use

As you use AceShiksha, we automatically store:

  • Quiz attempts, scores, and answers selected
  • Game scores and attempts (Maze, Car Race, History Timeline)
  • Lesson progress and AI feedback requests
  • Topic interactions and feedback responses
  • Leaderboard ranking data
  • Badges earned
  • PDF content you upload for AI quiz generation (processed in real time and not stored permanently)

3.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Your device's precise GPS location
  • Photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Contacts or call logs
  • Browsing history outside the App
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Payment or financial information (the App is free)

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Provide personalized learning content appropriate to your class level
  • Generate AI-powered quiz questions and lesson feedback using the Gemini API
  • Display quiz results, game scores, and leaderboards
  • Allow teachers to monitor their students' progress and performance
  • Generate student progress reports for teachers
  • Maintain and improve App functionality and stability
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling for commercial purposes, or any purpose unrelated to the educational function of the App.

5. AI-Powered Features (Gemini API)

AceShiksha uses Google's Gemini 2.5 Flash API to power:

  • AI lesson feedback and explanations
  • AI-generated quiz questions from PDF content
  • Quiz Battle and Rapid Fire question generation

When you use these features, the relevant text or PDF content is sent to Google's Gemini API for processing. This is subject to Google's own privacy policy and terms of service, available at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Important: PDF content you upload for quiz generation is sent to the Gemini API for real-time processing only. We do not store your uploaded PDFs in our database.

You are using an AI-powered service. AI responses are generated automatically and may occasionally be inaccurate. Always verify important information with your teacher.

6. Firebase Services

AceShiksha uses the following Google Firebase services:

  • Firebase Authentication: Manages account creation and login. Your password is never stored in plain text.
  • Cloud Firestore: Stores your profile data, quiz attempts, game scores, topics, and progress records. Data is stored in Google's asia-south1 (Mumbai) region.

Firebase is operated by Google LLC. Google's privacy policy governs Firebase data processing and is available at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

We do not use Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, Firebase Storage, Firebase Cloud Messaging, AdMob, or any other Firebase service beyond Authentication and Firestore.

7. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

We share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Google (Firebase / Gemini): As described in Sections 5 and 6 above, necessary for app operation.
  • With your teacher (within the App): Your quiz scores, game results, and progress reports are visible to the teacher associated with your class within the App. This is a core educational feature.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or government authority, we may disclose your information as legally obligated.
  • Safety: If we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the safety of any person or to address fraud or security issues.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App's services.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at nikhilsharma75612@gmail.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

Note: Deleting your account will permanently remove your profile, quiz history, game scores, and progress data. This action cannot be undone.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information:

  • Passwords are encrypted by Firebase Authentication and never stored in plain text
  • All data transmission between the App and Firebase/Gemini uses HTTPS encryption
  • Firestore security rules restrict data access so users can only access their own records
  • Teacher access is limited to their own students' data within the App

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by discontinuing use of the App

For any of the above requests, contact us at nikhilsharma75612@gmail.com.

Parents and guardians of users under 18 may exercise these rights on behalf of their child.

AceShiksha is an educational app used in school settings. Some users may be under 13 years of age. We take the following measures to protect younger users:

  • The App contains no advertisements
  • The App does not allow public posting or sharing of content outside the teacher-student relationship
  • We do not collect more data than is necessary for the educational purpose
  • Student data is only visible to their associated teacher within the App, not to other students
  • We do not use student data for any commercial purpose

If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child under 13 has registered without your consent, please contact us at nikhilsharma75612@gmail.com and we will delete the account immediately.

12. Permissions Used by the App

AceShiksha requests only the following device permissions:

  • Internet access: Required to connect to Firebase and the Gemini API
  • Read external storage (Android 9 and below) / Read media documents (Android 10+): Required only when you choose to upload a PDF for quiz generation

The App does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or any other sensitive device capability.
